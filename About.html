"Soul ties is a playlist that highlights the emotional bond between two people. Whether it's a hate that cuts deeper than any wound, or a love that goes beyond our physical body. Each song embodies what true love is, an emotional rollercoaster driven by souls who connect on a wavelength unlike any other. Like the moon and the tides, when you pull I love, and when you push I hate. The absence of our touch would set the world off balance like a broken dove. You are me and I am you; this connection can only be defined as one thing, Soul ties."
-Darius Fruge
